### CartLens

[![Website](https://img.shields.io/badge/Visit-cartlens.co-059669?style=flat-square&logo=googlechrome&logoColor=white)](https://cartlens.co)
[![X](https://img.shields.io/badge/X-@CartLensApp-000000?style=flat-square&logo=x&logoColor=white)](https://x.com/CartLensApp)

[![LinkedIn](https://img.shields.io/badge/LinkedIn-CartLens-0A66C2?style=flat-square&logo=linkedin&logoColor=white)](https://linkedin.com/company/cartlens-inc)
[![Reddit](https://img.shields.io/badge/Reddit-r%2FCartLens-FF4500?style=flat-square&logo=reddit&logoColor=white)](https://reddit.com/r/CartLens)
[![Product Hunt](https://img.shields.io/badge/Product_Hunt-CartLens-DA552F?style=flat-square&logo=producthunt&logoColor=white)](https://www.producthunt.com/products/cartlens)
[![Demo](https://img.shields.io/badge/YouTube-Full_Demo-FF0000?style=flat-square&logo=youtube&logoColor=white)](https://youtu.be/s5cTNW3cV8c)

<img src="https://www.cartlens.co/landing.jpg" alt="CartLens AI-powered receipt and shopping intelligence platform" width="100%" />

An AI-powered retail price-intelligence platform built to bring transparency to physical shopping.

CartLens analyzes receipts and price tags to help shoppers understand whether they received a fair price, compare local prices, and uncover opportunities to save.

**Core capabilities**

- AI-powered receipt and price-tag scanning
- Receipt OCR and automatic purchase digitization
- Crowdsourced local pricing intelligence
- Nearby price comparisons
- Personalized savings insights
- Historical purchase analysis
- CartAssistant for conversational purchase and market analysis
- Geospatial Lattice built from real, shopper-submitted prices
- Spending DNA, Optimization Scores, and leakage analysis
- Privacy-first, opt-in contribution to the crowdsourced price index

**Focus:** Applied AI · Computer Vision · Retail Intelligence · Crowdsourced Data · SaaS
